About Map

Welcome to the Informational Map of Leisure Parks within and around Salzburg City. This map is built to provide basic information on the location of leisure parks within and around the city of Salzburg for those considering recreational activities after the COVID-19 induced lockdown.

Zoom into your areas of Interest and check for information about the available parks you might want to visit

The map implements the Mouse-over effects, Get Lattitude of Clickable Areas and shows basic information about the parks with the use of Popups
